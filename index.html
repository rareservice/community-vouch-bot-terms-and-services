2. User Conduct & Rules

Vouching Integrity

Self-vouches are strictly prohibited. Any attempt to "fake" vouches or bypass the proof verification system will result in a permanent Global Ban from our network.

Scammer Policy

Users marked as "Scammer" or "Suspect" in our database will have restricted access. The Bot will automatically alert server owners when a flagged user joins.

Abuse Reporting

Filing false abuse reports or spamming the ticket system is grounds for an immediate blacklist. Proof is mandatory for all reports.

Invite Tracking

Using "fake" accounts or alt-bots to boost invite counts is detected and logged. We reserve the right to reset any user's invite statistics without notice.

3. Data & Privacy

We take data minimalization seriously. However, to provide our services, we store the following data in our Firebase Realtime Database:

  • Discord Identifiers: User IDs and Server IDs.
  • Vouch Content: Prize names, host names, and proof screenshots.
  • Invite Data: Logs of who invited which user to prevent join-leave spam.
  • Transcript Data: Message history of support/report tickets for staff review.

Note: We never store your personal real-life identity, IP addresses, or payment details directly.
